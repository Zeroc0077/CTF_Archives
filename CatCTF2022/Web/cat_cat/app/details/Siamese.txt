Siamese cat is a world famous short haired cat and a representative breed of short haired cat. The species originated in Siam (now Thailand), so it is named Siam cat. More than 200 years ago, this precious cat was raised only in Thailand's imperial palaces and big temples, and was a noble who stayed at home. Siamese cats can better adapt to the local climate of their owners, and they are strong, active, resourceful, curious and considerate
